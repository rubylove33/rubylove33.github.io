Goals

Start with a goal that feels worth keeping.

For people who want to stay close to a meaningful personal goal, not just check off another task.

  • Future writing helps you picture what matters.
  • Keywords and action planning turn the idea into direction.

Today

Keep the rhythm with one more small return.

The daily screen is designed for small, repeatable actions: a short note, a focus check, a reminder to stay connected.

  • Record progress without turning the app into pressure.
  • Gentle reminders help the goal stay present in daily life.

Library & Archive

Keep active goals and past goals separately on display.

Your current goals and previous goals are stored in different places, so the present path and the finished path can both stay visible.

  • Library holds the goals you are still carrying.
  • Archive keeps the goals you have already gone through.
  • You can observe the direction in which a goal evolves.

Review

It does not end with a single goal.

Futureseed puts special weight on looking back. With records and reviews, you can revisit how your goal changed, not just whether it was completed.

  • Small entries make change visible later.
  • Review helps you read the movement of the goal over time.
